A heartbroken clown, wayward souls, and juicy mechanical lips. As "The Big Event" draws near, help the troubled residents of the Habitat and de-mask the mysterious Doctor Habit ... before it's too late.


Smile For Me is an unconventional Puzzle Adventure about making people happy. In a curious world, you must rely on your nodding, shaking, and wits to get by.

Developed in a year by Gabe Lane and Yugo Limbo, Smile For Me tells a unique story of love, humanity, and forgiveness.
